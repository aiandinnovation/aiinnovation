About the Event

All the students of Anurag Engineering college are informed that the Department of CSE (AI & ML) is organizing an innovation event titled "AI and Innovation Sprints: Rapid Prototyping and Digital Transformation".

Students will work in teams to design and develop AI-based prototype solutions to solve real-world problems.

The objective is to encourage creativity, innovation, and application of Artificial Intelligence through rapid prototyping.
